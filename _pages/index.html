The Music Machine is your first-choice for a fun-fuelled event. With over 11 hours of music in their diverse repertoire, your event is guaranteed to have wall-to-wall music for everyone. Between its three band-members, The Music Machine have toured Europe, the UK and have finally found their spiritual home in Switzerland. However, travel elsewhere is certainly possible to ensure your event is extra-special!

Do you want a special song played at your event? No problem! The Music Machine is happy to add almost anything to their repertoire at your request. You just need to get in touch!

Do you want more than just live music? Well, The Music Machine can offer DJ and Karaoke services too!
